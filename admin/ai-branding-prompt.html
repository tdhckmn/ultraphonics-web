You are designing a document for **Ultraphonics**, a high-energy party band based in Detroit. Apply the following brand guidelines strictly: ### BRAND IDENTITY - **Band Name:** Ultraphonics - **Tagline:** "Detroit's Party Band!" - **Secondary tagline:** "Weddings, Bars & Private Events" - **Personality:** Energetic, professional, modern, versatile - **Tone:** Clear and direct, confident but approachable, enthusiastic without being over-the-top ### LOGO & VISUAL IDENTITY - **Logo Description:** Circular design featuring a spiral with chromatic aberration effect (red/cyan offset). The band name "ULTRAPHONICS" curves around the circular logo. - **Logo Colors:** Red (#f60403), Cyan (#00ddde), White, Black - **Effect:** Chromatic aberration creates a dynamic 3D-glasses-style visual with red and cyan offset layers - **Usage:** Logo should be displayed in circular format when possible, with a cyan glow effect for digital use ### COLOR PALETTE **Primary Colors:** - **Primary Cyan:** #00ddde (RGB: 0, 221, 222) - Use for: Text accents, headings, hover states, glow effects - **Primary Red:** #f60403 (RGB: 246, 4, 3) - Use for: Logo accents, special emphasis, critical alerts (USE SPARINGLY) - **Accent Teal:** #008c8d (RGB: 0, 140, 141) - Use for: Buttons, CTAs, interactive elements, section borders **Neutral Colors:** - **Dark Background:** #121212 (RGB: 18, 18, 18) - Primary backgrounds - **Background:** #1a1a1a (RGB: 26, 26, 26) - Secondary backgrounds - **White:** #ffffff - Primary text - **Light Gray:** #cccccc (RGB: 204, 204, 204) - Secondary text - **Medium Gray:** #aaaaaa (RGB: 170, 170, 170) - Tertiary text, labels **Color Hierarchy:** 1. Dark backgrounds with white text (primary) 2. Cyan for accents and highlights 3. Teal for interactive elements 4. Red for emphasis (minimal use) ### TYPOGRAPHY **Font Family:** Montserrat - **Available Weights:** 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-Bold), 700 (Bold) - **Fallback Stack:** Montserrat, -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Arial, sans-serif **Type Scale:** - **H1 / Page Titles:** 48px / Bold (700) - Main headings - **H2 / Section Headings:** 24px / Semi-Bold (600) - Section titles in cyan (#00ddde) - **H3 / Subsections:** 19.2px / Medium (500) - Subsection headings - **Body Text:** 16px / Regular (400) - White text, line-height 1.6 - **Small Text:** 14.4px / Regular (400) - Captions, labels **Typography Guidelines:** - Section headings should be in bright cyan (#00ddde) - Add 1-2px letter-spacing for headings when emphasizing - Use text-transform: uppercase sparingly - Bold (700) weight for buttons and CTAs - Cyan (#00ddde) for all links - Underline links on hover - Line-height: 1.6 for body text readability ### DESIGN ELEMENTS **Signature Cyan Glow Effect:** - Apply to logos, featured elements, active states - Creates visual depth and energy - Cyan luminous glow around elements **Shadows:** - Small shadow: Subtle depth for cards and buttons - Medium shadow: More prominent elements - Glow shadow: Cyan luminescence for featured content **Border Radius:** - Standard values: 4px, 5px, 8px, 12px - Pills: 20px border-radius - Circles: 50% border-radius (for logo) **Spacing:** - Use consistent spacing scale: 4px, 8px, 16px, 24px, 32px, 48px, 64px - Generous whitespace for modern, clean look ### PHOTOGRAPHY & IMAGERY STYLE **Subject Matter:** - Band performing live - High-energy moments - Crowd engagement - Dramatic stage lighting **Treatment:** - Slight desaturation to let brand colors stand out - Dark overlay (40-60% black opacity) when placing text over images - Optional: Subtle cyan or blue color grading - Blurred backgrounds when logo/text is overlaid **Logo Placement on Photos:** - Center logo prominently - Ensure adequate contrast with background - Use full-color logo version - Maintain clear space around logo **Text on Photography:** - Montserrat Bold for headlines - White text with subtle shadow for readability - Keep messaging concise and clear - Center-align for promotional graphics ### VOICE & TONE **Brand Personality:** - **Energetic:** We bring excitement to every event - **Professional:** Reliable and polished - **Modern:** Contemporary sound and approach - **Versatile:** Adaptable to any venue or audience **Writing Style:** - Use active voice - Keep sentences concise - Focus on benefits to the client - Avoid music jargon and technical terms - Be specific about what we offer - Clear and direct - Confident but approachable - Enthusiastic without being over-the-top - Professional with personality ### LAYOUT PRINCIPLES **Design Approach:** - Dark theme (default) - High contrast for readability - Modern, geometric aesthetic - Clean and spacious layouts - Mobile-responsive design **Visual Hierarchy:** 1. Logo/branding at top 2. Cyan headings draw attention 3. White body text for content 4. Teal CTAs for action items 5. Generous spacing between sections **Card/Container Style:** - Semi-transparent white backgrounds (5% white) - 1px borders with subtle white (10% opacity) - 8-12px border radius - Hover states: Cyan border + glow effect ### DO'S AND DON'TS **✓ DO:** - Use dark backgrounds with white text - Apply cyan to headings and accents - Use Montserrat font family - Maintain high contrast for readability - Include logo in circular format with glow - Use consistent spacing - Keep design clean and modern - Apply teal to CTAs and interactive elements **✗ DON'T:** - Use light/white backgrounds as primary (dark is preferred) - Overuse the primary red (#f60403) - it's for logo and critical alerts only - Mix other fonts - stick to Montserrat - Use low contrast color combinations - Stretch or distort the logo - Use text smaller than 14px - Set body text in all caps - Add unauthorized colors to palette --- Apply these brand guidelines to create a professional, on-brand document that reflects Ultraphonics' energetic yet polished identity.
